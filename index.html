Gold Medica Super Specialty Hospital, Bathinda

Gold Medica Super Speciality Hospital in Bathinda is a premier healthcare destination, renowned for its state-of-the-art facilities and exceptional medical expertise. With over 50 beds, the hospital stands as a beacon of advanced care in Neurosciences, Orthopaedics, Cardiac Sciences, Cancer Care, Obstetrics and Gynaecology, as well as Minimal Access, Metabolic, and Bariatric Surgery. The hospital boasts specialized units including a Chemo Day Care Unit, Surgical Intensive Care Unit (SICU), Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU), Paediatric Intensive Care Unit (PICU), and a Medical Intensive Care Unit (MICU), alongside a cutting-edge Cath Lab. Equipped with 10 ICU beds and 4 high-end modular Operation Theatres, Gold Medica ensures precision and efficiency in every procedure. What truly sets us apart is the guidance of over 30 experienced doctors and medical experts, supported by a dedicated team of 140+ skilled nurses. Together, we are committed to delivering world-class healthcare in a compassionate and patient-centric environment. At Gold Medica Super Speciality Hospital, we blend the latest in medical technology with unwavering care, ensuring every patient receives the highest standard of treatment. Your health is our priority, and we are here to serve you with excellence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Discover answers to the most commonly asked questions about our specialties, services, facilities, appointment bookings, and patient care.

1. What specialties and services does the hospital offer?

We provide a wide range of super-specialty services, including cardiology, neurology, oncology, orthopedics, gastroenterology, nephrology, and more. Our expert doctors are supported by advanced diagnostic and treatment facilities.

4. How do I book an appointment with a specialist?

Appointments can be booked via our website, or by calling our appointment helpline. Walk-in patients are also accommodated based on availability.

5. What are the visiting hours for patients?

Our visiting hours are from 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM on weekdays and 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM on weekends. Visitors must adhere to hospital rules and guidelines to ensure patient safety.

6. Is emergency care available 24/7?

Yes, our emergency department is open 24/7. We are fully equipped with advanced technology and staffed by trained emergency specialists to handle all critical situations.

7. Does the hospital offer diagnostic and laboratory services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive diagnostic and laboratory services, including MRI, CT scan, X-ray, blood tests, and other advanced diagnostics, all available within the hospital.
